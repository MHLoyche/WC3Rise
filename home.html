This website is created for a warcraft 3 map called Rise.
Rise is a dungeoncrawl map, where you have to journey through various terrains and fight multiple difficult bosses.
The map entails an intricate item system and custom made abilities, where you have to work together with your team to overcome the challenges.
You can also do the ultimate challenge of overcoming the map on a solo adventure, to prove that you are indeed a superior gamer.
This map is created by Rel!chunter and is still receiving frequent updates.

You can join the community on discord by clicking the discord image.
Here you can provide feedback and communicate with the developer of the map.
